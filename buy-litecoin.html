How to Buy Litecoin

This guide shows you the safest and easiest way to buy Litecoin and move it into your wallet.

Step 1: Choose where to buy

You can buy Litecoin through exchanges or directly inside some wallets. Choose a trusted platform.

Step 2: Buy a small amount first

Always start small. This helps you learn the process without risk.

Step 3: Send to your wallet

After buying, send your Litecoin to your wallet using your receive address or QR code.

Step 4: Confirm the transaction

Wait for confirmations. Once confirmed, your Litecoin is safely in your wallet.

What to avoid

Do not leave large amounts on exchanges. Always move it to your own wallet.
