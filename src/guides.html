Guide 1

How to Set Up a Litecoin Wallet

Keep wallet education simple, secure, and beginner-friendly.

Nexus Wallet and Cake Wallet

  1. Choose a wallet that supports Litecoin.
  2. Download the wallet from the official source.
  3. Create your wallet and back up the recovery phrase.
  4. Do not share your seed phrase or private keys.
  5. Use your Litecoin address or QR code to receive funds.

Guide 2

How to Buy Litecoin

Help users understand the safest path from purchase to wallet custody.

  1. Set up your wallet first.
  2. Use a supported wallet buy feature or a trusted exchange.
  3. Review fees before confirming the purchase.
  4. Move your Litecoin into your own wallet if needed.
  5. Double-check the receiving address before sending.
Guide 3

How to Send & Receive Litecoin

Make transactions less intimidating for new users.

  1. Open your Litecoin wallet.
  2. To receive, copy your address or show your QR code.
  3. To send, paste the destination address or scan a QR code.
  4. Enter the amount carefully.
  5. Review the details and confirm the transaction.
Guide 4

How to Accept Litecoin for Your Business

Keep the merchant setup path clear and simple.

  1. Choose whether you want direct wallet payments or a payment processor.
  2. Set up BitPay or your preferred payment solution.
  3. Create a checkout flow using a QR code, online checkout, or invoice.
  4. Test the payment process before going live.
  5. Train staff on how to recognize and complete a payment.
